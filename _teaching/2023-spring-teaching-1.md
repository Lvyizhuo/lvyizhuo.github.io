During this period, I served as a Mathematics teacher, gaining valuable experience in communicating complex concepts in a simplified manner.

## Responsibilities
---
* **Curriculum Delivery**: Responsible for the mathematics education of primary school students, ensuring alignment with national education standards.
* **Simplifying Complexity**: Transformed abstract mathematical concepts into digestible and engaging lessons for young learners.
* **Assessment & Feedback**: Evaluated student progress through regular testing and provided personalized feedback to improve learning outcomes.

## Key Skills Gained
---
* **Communication**: Enhanced the ability to explain logic-heavy topics (math) to a non-technical audience.
* **Organization**: Managed lesson plans and classroom schedules effectively over a six-month period.
* **Leadership**: Guided students through foundational mathematical logic, fostering their interest in STEM subjects.
